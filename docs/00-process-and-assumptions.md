## Primary goal: drive conversion — and the key changes that serve it

**The north star for this refresh is conversion, not awareness.** The page exists to move a Monetization/Growth PM from "stale, generic monetization vendor" to a self-serve **"Start for free"** signup (enterprise served in parallel by "Talk to sales"). Every structural change below was chosen because it removes friction or de-risks a decision moment on the path to that action — not because it's a nicer story for its own sake. The single number to optimize is **self-serve signup conversion rate** (Track 6).

**Key conversion-driving changes made (consolidated from Tracks 4 → 5 → 6):**
- **Promoted self-serve above the fold.** The live hero leads with the sales-gated **"Request a demo"**; we promote **"Start for free"** to the dominant hero CTA (sales demoted to co-located secondary). The release-latency-blocked PM's highest-intent next step is to *try it now*, not book a call a week out — and the free-to-$2,500-MTR model means there's no commercial reason to gate them.
- **Price-honest microcopy at the click.** *"Free until $2,500 in monthly tracked revenue"* printed under every primary CTA — pre-empts the cost objection at the exact moment it would stall an SMB PM.
- **Objection answered next to the CTA it stalls.** Build-vs-buy ("we'll build it in-house") gets its own section with the primary CTA *immediately after* the concede-then-reframe — the tightest objection→action pairing on the page.
- **Proof at every decision moment.** Each value section pairs its claim with a verified outcome stat right before its CTA (hero scale strip → hard-paywall benchmark → Floga → Pixelcut → build-vs-buy heavy proof → closing logo wall), so the riskiest moment is the best-evidenced one.
- **Killed the conversion-leaking patterns.** Cut the 5× vague "Learn more" team-role CTAs, the 7-bullet IAP feature grid (laundry list, no momentum), and the dev-onboarding "How it works" / 5-persona router that dilute PM focus and altitude.
- **Contextual CTAs that carry the verb.** "Ship your first paywall free" / "Run your first experiment free" — action + value at each section, same self-serve destination.
